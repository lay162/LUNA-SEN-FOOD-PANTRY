When you buy a custom domain for this site, update these so links, SEO and QR codes stay correct:

1. Netlify (or your host): add the custom domain and set environment variable
   VITE_SITE_URL=https://your-domain.co.uk
   Then trigger a new deploy.

2. index.html (in the project root): set absolute URLs to your domain for
   - og:url
   - twitter:url
   - JSON-LD script: url, logo, image

3. public/robots.txt: Sitemap line — use your real domain.

4. Optional: src/constants/site.js documents the same checklist for developers.
